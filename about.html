NYNX - Record and Promotion Label

NYNX is a small, independent Promotion and Record Label founded in 2019. NYNX provides unique and future-headed bass music with new and creative ideas. We support our Members with Promotions all over the Internet and offer a completely free Membership in our Label. We won't take anything from our Artist's Income and Members will keep 100% of their money.
